1. Data Collection: We Collect Nothing

We believe in privacy. Our app is designed to be a simple, offline tool.

No Personal Information

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. "Personal information" includes your name, email address, phone number, location, device identifiers, or any other data that could be used to identify you.

No Usage Data

We do not collect any information about how you use the app (analytics), what you calculate, or the data you enter (such as your water or electricity consumption figures).

Local Calculations

All calculations performed by the App happen exclusively on your device. The information you enter for your bill calculations never leaves your device and is not visible to us.

Since we do not collect any data, we do not use any data for any purpose. This includes:
