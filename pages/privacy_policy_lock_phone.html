Privacy Policy Lock phone

Last updated: February 17, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Lock Phone ("we", "our", or "us") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This app is designed to help you focus by locking your phone for a set duration.

2. Data Collection and Usage

Key Point: We do NOT collect, store, or share any of your personal data.

Lock Phone operates entirely offline on your device. We do not have servers, and we do not transmit any data to third parties.

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or contacts.
  • No Usage Data: We do not track how you use other apps or websites.
  • No Location Data: We do not access or track your location.

3. Sensitive Permissions Breakdown

To function effectively as a focus tool, Lock Phone requires specific sensitive permissions. Here is exactly why we need them and how they are used:

A. Accessibility Service API

This app uses the Accessibility Service API to detect when you attempt to use other apps while the "Lock" mode is active.

  • Why is it needed? To prevent you from opening distracted apps (like social media or games) during your focus session. If you try to leave the Lock Phone screen, the service detects this event and immediately brings you back to the lock screen.
  • Data Handling: The service acts purely as a monitor for window state changes. It does not read, store, or transmit any content from your screen, text you type, or buttons you press in other apps (except to detect the "Got it" button on the system pinning dialog to facilitate the lock mode).

B. Device Administrator Permission

This app requests Device Administrator permission.

  • Why is it needed? This permission is strictly used to prevent the app from being uninstalled while a lock session is active. This ensures that you cannot bypass the timer by simply deleting the app.
  • Data Handling: This permission does not give us access to your data; it only invokes a system-level restriction on uninstallation during the lock period.

C. Draw Over Other Apps (Overlay)

This app may display over other apps.

  • Why is it needed? To ensure the lock screen remains visible and cannot be easily dismissed by pressing the home or back buttons on some devices.

4. Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics or advertising services (such as Google Analytics, Firebase, or AdMob) that collect user data.

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
