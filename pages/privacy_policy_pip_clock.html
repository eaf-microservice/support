Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

Types of Data Collected

Permissions Usage

Our Application requires specific permissions to function correctly. These are used solely for the core functionality of the app:

  • Notifications: Required to play the hourly chime and keep the clock active in the background. On Android, this displays a persistent foreground service notification. On iOS, this allows scheduled alerts.
  • Ignore Battery Optimizations: On Android, we may request this to prevent the system from killing the background clock service, ensuring accurate timekeeping.
