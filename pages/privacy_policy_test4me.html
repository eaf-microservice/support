Privacy Policy - Test For Me application

Last Updated: April 25, 2026

Welcome to Test for me. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you use our application and tell you about your privacy rights.

1. The Data We Collect

When you use "Test for me", we may collect, use, and store different kinds of personal data about you, which we have grouped together follows:

  • Identity Data: includes your name, username, or Google profile information (if you sign in using Google).
  • Contact Data: includes your email address.
  • App Data: includes the names, package names, and Play Store links of the applications you submit for testing.
  • Usage Data: includes information about your participation in testing groups, your role (Admin or Tester), and your progress.

2. How We Use Your Data

We will only use your personal data when the law allows us to. Most commonly, we will use your personal data in the following circumstances:

  • To register you as a new user.
  • To facilitate the core functionality of the app, which is connecting app developers with testers to fulfill Google Play Console's 20-tester requirement.
  • To manage your relationship with us, including notifying you about changes to our terms or privacy policy.
  • To send you push notifications regarding group updates or testing requirements.

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your Personally Identifiable Information to outside parties. However, we use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Play Services & Firebase: We use Firebase Authentication and Firestore to manage user accounts and store app data securely.

Other users of the app will be able to see your testing group details (if you choose to share your group in the community) and your email address if they are part of the same testing group to facilitate the testing process.

4. Data Security

We have put in place appropriate security measures to prevent your personal data from being accidentally lost, used, or accessed in an unauthorized way, altered, or disclosed. All data is securely stored using Google Firebase infrastructure.

5. Your Legal Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data.
  • Request correction of the personal data that we hold about you.
  • Request erasure of your personal data (Account Deletion).

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at via our Google Play Store developer contact email.
