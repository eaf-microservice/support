1. Information We Handle

Our app is designed with your privacy as a priority. To perform its function, the app needs to handle the phone number you wish to message.

Phone Numbers

When you enter a phone number to start a chat, the App handles this information temporarily on your device.

  • We do not save, store, or record the phone number in the App.
  • We do not transmit the phone number to our servers. We don't have servers.
  • We do not share the phone number with any third party other than the official WhatsApp application on your device.

No Personal Information

Other than the temporary handling of the phone number, we do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. "Personal information" includes your name, email address, location, device identifiers, or any other data that could be used to identify you.

No Usage Data

We do not collect any information about how you use the app (analytics), what you send, or the data you enter (such as message or phone number).

2. Data Usage

Since we do not collect any data, we do not use any data for any purpose. This includes:

  • We do not use your data for advertising.
  • We do not use your data for analytics.
  • We do not sell or share your data with anyone.

3. Interaction with WhatsApp

Our App's core function is to make it easier to start a conversation on WhatsApp.

  • The App takes the phone number you provide and uses the official WhatsApp service to open a chat.
  • The App itself does not send the message or store your contacts. You are always in control of sending the message using the official WhatsApp application.
  • Once you are redirected to WhatsApp, your activity, communication, and data are governed by WhatsApp's Privacy Policy. We strongly encourage you to review their policy to understand how they handle your data.

4. Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without verification of parental consent, we will take steps to remove that information.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the App or on our app store listing. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
