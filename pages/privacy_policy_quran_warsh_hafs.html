Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is handled in the Quran Karim Offline application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

The App is designed to be used offline and does not collect any personal information from you. All data is stored locally on your device.

The App stores the following information on your device using the shared_preferences package:

  • Night Mode: Your preference for a dark or light theme.
  • Reading Mode: Your preferred reading mode (Warsh or Hafs).
  • Last Page: The last page you were reading to allow you to resume your reading.
  • Bookmarks: A list of pages you have bookmarked for future reference.

This information is stored solely on your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information stored on your device is used solely to enhance your experience with the App. We do not have access to this information.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not share your information with any third parties. The App includes a "Share" feature that allows you to share a link to the App's page on the Google Play Store. This action is initiated by you, and no personal data is shared in the process.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information stored on your device. However, since the data is stored locally, the security of your device is your responsibility.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take the necessary actions.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in this section of the App.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us.
